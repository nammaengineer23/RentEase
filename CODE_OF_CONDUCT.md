# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for everyone contributing to RentEase.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and professional.
- Welcome differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully.
- Focus on what is best for the community.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discriminatory conduct.
- Personal attacks or insulting comments.
- Disruptive or abusive behavior in discussions or contributions.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, and pull requests that violate this Code of Conduct.
